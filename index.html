ABOUT

What is Boston outdoor film festival?

BOSTON OUTDOOR FILM FESTIVAL is an annual event hosted in Boston Public Garden, a park in Boston city, shows diversity of long and short films every night for four nights.

This year we have 1500 seats are available to all ages for free. Your entertainment is our mission and we aim to give enjoyable time.

Who are we?

Watch it outside events Co. is an event's organizer company. Founded by Clara Dunn and her awesome team. We have participated in popular events and fesitvals all over the city. Our goal is to provide a remarkable experience for the audience.
